Refund Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2023

Digital Products

Due to the nature of digital products, which cannot be returned like physical goods, all sales of our downloadable digital products are considered final. Once a digital product has been purchased and the download link has been provided, we do not offer refunds.

Exceptions

We value your satisfaction and understand that exceptions may be necessary in certain circumstances. We may consider refund requests for the following situations:

  • Technical Issues: If you experience technical issues that prevent you from accessing or downloading your purchased product, and our support team is unable to resolve these issues within 7 days of your purchase.
  • Product Not as Described: If the product's content is substantially different from what was described at the time of purchase.

Refund Request Process

To request a refund under the exceptions listed above, please contact our support team at support@valueguides.co within 7 days of your purchase. Your request should include:

  • Your order number
  • The email address used for the purchase
  • A detailed explanation of why you're requesting a refund
  • Any relevant supporting information

We will review your request and respond within 5 business days. If your refund request is approved, the refund will be processed to the original method of payment within 10 business days.

100% Money-Back Guarantee

For select products marked with our "100% Money-Back Guarantee," you may request a refund if you're not satisfied with the product within 30 days of purchase. This guarantee applies only to products explicitly marked as such.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Refund Policy, please contact us at support@valueguides.co.
